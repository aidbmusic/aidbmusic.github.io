Volcano and Heart

信息

表演者: The Coral Sea

专辑类型: 专辑

介质: CD

发行时间: 2005-01-01

出版者: Red Clover

唱片数: 1

条形码: 0641444964123

豆瓣评分: 8.7

简介

　　又是一支走英式路线的Indie乐队，但不同的是这支乐队来自美国，一支美国乐队能把英式玩的如此出色，倒是不得不引起人的注意，但事实上这也并不乏先例，Ambulance LTD，The Bravery等乐队都是把英式玩的异常出色的美国乐队的典范，这年头风格和地区的界限已经越来越模糊了。

　　这张专辑可能在同类作品中并不算最出色的，但它依然值得你去好好聆听，主唱细腻清澈的嗓音会让你一下就记住这支乐队，歌曲编排以及旋律都十分出色。in between the days是首比较偏民谣的舒缓情歌，也是专辑中最让人感动的一首歌，适合在夜晚安静的聆听，抛开一切世俗杂念，完全沉浸在木吉他清脆的扫弦中；lake and ocean是英式味道浓重的一首作品，前奏干净的吉他声让我对这首歌充满期待，而当主唱嗓音响起的时候，我差点以为这就是山羊皮，没错，主唱的嗓音尽管没有Brett Anderson那般阴阳怪气和神经质，但却足够细腻和伤感；ancient modern people是专辑中比较重的一首歌，嘶哑的吉他声很有金属感，像极了山羊皮，或者是那个年代的一些老英伦乐队。

Under The Iron Sea

信息

表演者: Keane

流派: 摇滚

专辑类型: 专辑

介质: Audio CD

发行时间: 2006

出版者: Interscope Records

唱片数: 1

条形码: 0602498568279 ISRC(中国): CNC180730500

豆瓣评分: 8.1

简介

　　《Under The Iron Sea》终于来了，等得还真是辛苦，先不说等得时间长，就说这期间被假专辑骗的次数都让人受不了，各种下载方式都有，BT,emule,http,Ftp。只有你想不到的，没有国外那些骗子做不到的。好了，不说他们了，来看看我们今天的正版货。Keane 新专辑《Under The Iron Sea》，历时俩年的制作，将要在12号和20号分别在英国和美国发行。新专辑共11首歌曲，其实在这之前我们拿到的曲目是12首，而且是官方给的曲目，但为什么又少一首呢？原因很简单，你听过之后就会明白，Put It Behind You和The Iron Sea 被放在了一起，而The Iron Sea 则是2分钟的纯曲子，可以算是隐藏曲吧。专辑开篇的是Atlantic，我听到这首歌曲的时候是在5月拿到Music Video，这只MV的制作也大有来头，是由苏格兰作家Irvine Welsh导演的，一只带有带有灰色低迷调性的音乐录影带。Is It Any Wonder 就不用说了，之前大家就已经听过了，现在放到专辑中去听，还真觉得Is It Any Wonder是那么特别，专辑中最乍眼的歌曲，宏大的旋律。Nothing In My Way会让我找到以前Somewhere Only We Know 影子，俩首歌曲不说十分相似，但也有着微微的关联。专辑难免会让你听到有吉他的声音，但其实那些都是钢琴做出来的效果，其实在他们制作专辑的时候钢琴手Tim对nme.com说：“吉他也不是绝对不用，因为Tom的吉他也弹得不错。在很早的时候，我们确实有想过用一些吉他的可能，但我们从没有时间去做这件事。”最终他们仍然否定了吉他的使用，并且用钢琴和音效做出了非常榜的音效。像Put It Behind You和Crystal Ball都是很好的体现。A Bad Dream的演唱和演唱方式很是吸引我，很有韵味。The Frong Prince开始的有些水聆，Try Again则是那种忧郁的慢歌。这张专辑很饱满，可以说有一切你想要Keane的东西，也有你未尝知道的东西在其中。突破以往《 Hopes and Fears》的风格… 赞。

Tonight: Franz Ferdinand

信息

表演者: Franz Ferdinand

流派: 摇滚

专辑类型: Import

介质: Audio CD

发行时间: 2009-01-27

出版者: Sony

唱片数: 2

条形码: 0886973725511

豆瓣评分: 7.7

简介

　　Franz Ferdinand第三张专辑《Tonight: Franz Ferdinand》整整在录音室酝酿了一年多的时间，其主要原因是他们想尝试全新的音乐风格，希望能在这张专辑中吸收更多的音乐元素，所以这是Franz Ferdinand非常用功的作品，值得细细品味。

　　Tonight: Franz Ferdinand》就像专辑名字一样，给人的第一感觉有一些40、50年代夜总会的味道，专辑中很多歌曲都受吸收了舞池音乐的精华，显得格外的享乐主义，其中也不乏受到一些非洲音乐元素的影响，会让人有一种跟着节奏摇摆的冲动，富有激情，让人心跳加速，犹如酒精在血液中一样让人兴奋，甚至是亢奋。不光如此，最后一首“Katherine Kiss Me”也算是个180度的大转弯，清新的吉他，深情的男生独唱，给人一种别样的冲击力，完美收场。

唱片数: 1

豆瓣评分: 7.5

简介

　　The King of Limbs is the eighth studio album by English alternative rock band Radiohead, produced by Nigel Godrich. It will be released on 19 February 2011 as a download in MP3 and WAV formats, followed by a physical CD release in the UK on 28 March [1] and a special “newspaper” edition on 9 May 2011.[2] The “newspaper” edition will contain “two clear 10-inch vinyl records in a purpose-built record sleeve, many large sheets of artwork, 625 tiny pieces of artwork, a compact disc, and a full-colour piece of oxo-degradable plastic to hold it all together”.[3]

　　The album was announced on Radiohead’s website on 14 February 2011, five days before release.[4] The name of the album possibly refers to an oak tree in Wiltshire’s Savernake Forest, thought to be 1,000 years old.[4] The tree is a pollarded oak, referring to an ancient technique for harvesting timber for fencing and firewood. Though it does not feature on maps, the tree is said to be 3 miles (4.8 km) from Tottenham Court House, where Radiohead recorded part of their previous album In Rainbows.[5]

简介

　　the sixth full-length release by the Manchester indie rock group James, was produced by Brian Eno during the same six-week session as Laid. Although the group had always been keen on improvisation, both as their principal songwriting method and onstage, this was their first (and, thus far, only) album to be comprised entirely of such material. It was Eno’s idea that the group’s improvisations should be recorded, having told them that he regarded this music as having equal importance to the finished songs birthed from it. However, the group told Stuart Maconie (for their authorised biography Folklore) that the album caused disagreements, particularly over Tim Booth’s wish to redo some vocals and over how to deal with the finished record - originally planned as a giveaway with early copies of Laid, it was eventually issued as a limited edition several months later.

　　One track, Jam-J was chosen as a single, and released as a double-A side, along with “Say Something” from the album Laid.

　　The track “Tomorrow” was later re-recorded from scratch and placed on the band’s next release, the album Whiplash as well as being culled as the second single from that record.

简介

　　The Cribs have released a lo-fi VHS-style video for their pulsing new track ‘Leather Jacket Love Song’ to celebrate their forthcoming singles collection ‘Payola’ released February 25th.

　　Directed in the band’s hometown of Wakefield, the video intersperses camcorder footage from 1997 with newly shot footage to show the current day Cribs performing with their teenage selves.

　　Director Andy Knowles said: “The video uses a sophisticated mix of visual effects, hidden cuts, lookalikes and set decoration to sell the illusion that two generations of Cribs are performing together at the same time.”

　　Originally written for the band’s fourth album, ‘Ignore The Ignorant’ (released September 9th 2009), ‘Leather Jacket Love Song’ is an infectious slice of nostalgic indie-pop and remains one of their most sought after tracks.

　　The band will play London’s Brixton Academy on February 22nd and additional cuts from the video can be viewed at leatherjacketlovesong.com.

　　‘Leather Jacket Love Song’ is set to be released on February 11th.
