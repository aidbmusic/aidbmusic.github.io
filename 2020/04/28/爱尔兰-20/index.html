简介

　　Review by Stephen Thomas Erlewine

　　From the outset, U2 went for the big message — every song on their debut album Boy sounds huge, with oceans of processed guitars cascading around Bono’s impassioned wail. It was an inspired combination of large, stadium-rock beats and post-punk textures. Without the Edge’s echoed, ringing guitar, U2 would have sounded like a traditional hard rock band, since the rhythm section and Bono treat each song as an anthem. Of course, that’s the charm of Boy: all of its emotions are on the surface, delivered with optimistic, youthful self-belief, yet the unusual, distinctive guitar textures give it an unexpected tension that makes it an exhilarating debut. The songs may occasionally show some weakness — the driving “I Will Follow,” the dark “An Cat Dubh,” and the shimmering “The Ocean” stand out among the sonic textures — yet the band’s musical and lyrical vision keep Boy compelling until the finish.

　　

　　From www.allmusic.com

Songs Of Experience

信息

表演者: U2

流派: 摇滚

专辑类型: 专辑

介质: Audio CD

发行时间: 2017-12-01

出版者: Interscope

唱片数: 1

条形码: 0602557976991

豆瓣评分: 7.4

简介

　　★ 全球冲破1亿7000万销售天量、22座葛莱美加持，地表最伟大摇滚天团

　　★ 延续冠军专辑【赤子之心Songs Of Innocence】全新续篇

　　★ 收录告示牌摇滚榜TOP5主打”You ‘re The Best Thing About Me”等13首力作

　　坐拥22座葛莱美奖、7座全英音乐奖以及2座金球奖，全球1亿7000万张销售、7张告示牌冠军专辑，受封摇滚名人堂成员及滚石杂志册封「史上最伟大音乐人」等无数荣耀头衔，U2不仅仅缔造了前所未有的丰功伟业，更在乐史上留下了不可抹灭的地位；仅管如此他们却从未停下脚步，依旧不断开创出全新音乐版图，让世人一次又一次接受U2的音乐洗礼。2017年，U2延续前作【Songs Of Innocence】的音乐概念，再次蕴酿出生涯最具突破性、最大胆创新的革命新作【Songs Of Experience】！

　　2014年9月9日，U2首度与Apple合作将第13张专辑【Songs Of Innocence】发送给全世界超过5亿用户，替数字音乐时代写下全新里程碑，缔造了「史上最多人在专辑发行首日就能同步拥有一张专辑」的空前纪录；睽违了3年，U2延续前作的概念和名称，发行阔别已久的第14张专辑【Songs of Experience】，两张专辑的灵感同样来自于18世纪英国最伟大的诗人之一William Blake的画诗集，代表了从纯真和批判之中，挑战了后工业社会的弊病。而U2选择挪用这样的伟大意境，加上他们独到创作概念，透过旋律和节奏重新建构，针对21世纪的科技造成社会疏离，透过音乐提出抨击与建言，也同时为他们在乐坛塑造出全新高度。主唱Bono表示「这个世界已完全变了，我们需要停下来好好思考，并且审视这波变化。同时，伴随着许多政治选举，欧美洲都面临了前所未有的挑战，而我也看到很多人遭逢苦难，我想要把这些都写进歌里，与大家谈谈这些事。」也就是因为怀抱如此宏大的愿景，以及他们对于抵抗不公不义的坚持，U2在Bono带领之下，再次交出了万众期盼的全新专辑【Songs of Experience】。

　　【Songs of Experience】有请葛莱美奖认证的共和世代Onerepublic主唱Ryan Tedder(爱黛儿、碧昂丝)、金曲制造机Jacknife Lee(泰勒丝)等人连手操刀，替U2灌注最顶尖的节奏脉动；首波主打”You’re the Best Thing About Me”，不仅空降告示牌热门摇滚单曲榜No.5，融合了摇滚与主流韵味的歌曲氛围，透过深邃歌词的搭配，呈现出U2对于人性、家庭与亲情的刻画，展现了独步全球的摇滚意境；”Get Out of Your Own Way”则是以鼓励和批判并行的模式，带给聆听者极大的启发，并在歌词中透露「人必须勇于挑战自我」的概念。沉重的世代需要伟大的音乐来歌颂，而【Songs of Experience】肯定就是这个全新世代的经典杰作。

出版者: Revive Records

唱片数: 1

条形码: 0793573799524

豆瓣评分: 8.4

简介

　　God Is an Astronaut are an instrumental three piece hailing from the Glen of the Downs, Co. Wicklow, Ireland. They formed in 2002 and released their debut album The End of the Beginning the same year on their own Revive Records label. Their two music videos for The End of the Beginning and From Dust to the Beyond, received airplay on MTV UK and on other MTV European networks. All Is Violent, All Is Bright (2005) is the band’s second album. The album features their build ups from serene ambience to searing intensity and is a closer representation of their live sound. The single Fragile from the album has also received plays on MTV2 UK’s 120 minute show and MTV’s “The Comedown” show. God Is an Astronaut released an EP called A Moment Of Stillness in 2006. Their third album, Far From Refuge, was released in April 2007 on Revive Records and as a download via their website at http://www.superadmusic.com/god/. They released a self titled album in 2008, and plan to release their fifth album, Age of the Fifth Sun in the summer of 2010.

Songs of Innocence

信息

表演者: U2

流派: 摇滚

专辑类型: 专辑

介质: Audio CD

发行时间: 2014-10-14

出版者: Island Records

唱片数: 1

条形码: 0602547048929

豆瓣评分: 7.6

简介

　　https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/songs-of-innocence/id915794155

　　Today’s Apple Event in Cupertino, California saw the launch of the new iPhone 6, Apple’s digital wallet, and iWatch. The real headliner, however, came at the end when U2 appeared as a special guest. After debuting a new song live, the band announced that their latest album, Songs of Innocence, is being released for free on iTunes starting today. Yes, they simultaneously pulled a Beyoncé and Radiohead.

　　Available to all of Apple’s 500 million customers free of charge, it marks the biggest album release of all time. Songs of Innocence will remain exclusive to Apple through October.

　　According to Rolling Stone, Innocence was primarily produced by Danger Mouse with additional help from Joshua Tree collaborator Flood. In an interview, Bono said the group listened to music they loved in the Seventies, referencing David Bowie, Joy Division, and The Ramones. In fact, the lead track is called “The Miracle (of Joey Ramone)”. Another track, “This Is Where You Can Reach Me Now”, is a tribute to The Clash. Meanwhile, Lykke Li appears on the track “The Troubles”.

　　A physical release for the record is set for October 13th. A deluxe edition includes acoustic session of songs from the album and four additional tracks: “Lucifer’s Hands”, “The Crystal Ballroom”, “The Troubles (Alternative version)” and “Sleep Like a Baby Tonight (Alternative Perspective Mix by Tchad Blake)”. A double white-vinyl LP will also be available.

简介

　　Winter Poem is the 2011 album by Secret Garden. Primarily an instrumental album, it features three songs with guest vocals: Moya Brennan of Clannad fame on “The Dream”, Fionnuala Gill on “Mary’s Lament”, and Tracey Campbell and Espen Grjotheim on “Powered By Nature”.
