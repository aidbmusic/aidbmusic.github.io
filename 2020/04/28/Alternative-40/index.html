Beautiful Lies

信息

又名: 美丽谎言

发行时间: 2016-03-25

唱片数: 1

其他版本: Beautiful Lies (Deluxe) （全部）

简介

　　＊睽违近3年的全新创作，Birdy出道至今最贴近心灵、脱茧而出的成年之作！

　　＊Adele、Arctic Monkeys、Lana Del Rey、Maroon 5等超强制作创作班底联手赋予全新的音乐温度，征服所有挑剔的耳朵！

　　＊收录已率先拿下KKBOX排行冠军的首支单曲「Keeping Your Head Up」，温柔与坚强并蓄的「Wild Horses」！

　　一头微卷长发，自然散发出一抹超龄的气质，来自英国的漂亮女孩Birdy，超龄的醇厚嗓音中蕴藏无比穿透力，成熟态势中依附着孤傲灵魂，凝聚出难以言喻的绝对魅力。 Birdy在7岁即开始接触钢琴、8岁开始尝试音乐创作，国小六年级时就带着自创曲「So Be Free」一路过关斩将，赢得英国歌唱竞赛节目Open Mic UK之18岁以下分组冠军，顺利签下一纸合约。胆识过人的Birdy，以新秀之姿出道，顶着创作人身分，于2011年问市首张同名专辑“Birdy”，却推翻传统选择发行翻唱特辑，将Bon Iver、Cherry Ghost、Phoenix、The National 、The XX等乐团歌手名作，转化成独树一帜的凄美，摘下多国排行后冠，全球销售150万张，其中翻作Bon Iver的「Skinny Love」更在全球大卖400万张，散尽触碰心灵的真挚情怀。 2013年发行个人全创作专辑“Fire Within”横扫欧洲各国排行Top10，其中与OneRepublic的Ryan Tedder合作的主打单曲「Wings」也拿下10国单曲榜Top 10。此外，Birdy的创作才华也备受瞩目，短短几年中就陆续受邀为电影【饥饿游戏】、【生命中的美好缺憾】谱写演唱歌曲、并与英国乐团Mumford & Sons在动画【勇敢传说】中共同合作，同时也在巨星DJ大卫库塔专辑中献声，期间更荣获2014德国ECHO音乐大奖「最佳国际女艺人」、西班牙音乐大奖Los Premios 40 Principales 2014「最佳国际新进艺人」头衔，更受到2014全英音乐奖「最佳英国女歌手」、第55届葛莱美「最佳影视媒体作品歌曲奖」入围肯定！

　　Birdy从首张专辑演绎自己钟爱的歌曲来寻找自己身为歌手出发的定位，再藉由首张全创作专辑“Fire Within”正式起飞、展现音乐才华，打出一片属于自己的舞台。相隔近3年，现年19岁的Birdy，经历一连串的音乐洗礼后，在她即将步入双十年华之际推出个人第三张专辑“Beautiful Lies”，邀请到也才年仅21岁的女摄影师Oliva Bee来为其新专辑封面内页视觉概念操刀。 Oliva曾受邀为Converse、Hermes、Adidas等知名品牌拍摄，英国卫报更称赞其作品实验味道浓厚、拍摄出如梦般的意境。 Birdy因为阅读艺伎回忆录这本书才因此想在新专辑中实现日本东方色彩的概念，而透过Olivia的镜头，确实拍摄出Birdy那交织着坚韧孤傲的冷艳形象。专辑歌曲的创作雏型自2014年Birdy在美国巡回期间就已形成，后期于英国完成词曲创作，除了前两张专辑的制作人伙伴Jim Abbiss (Adele, Arctic Monkeys)再度受邀外，另请到Al Shux (Tine Tempah, Lana Del Rey)、Steve Mac (James Blunt, One Direction)、MyRiot (London Grammar)、名混音师Craig Silvey (Arcade Fire, Florence and The Machine)分别赋予“Beautiful Lies”不同的音乐温度，而Birdy也首次实际参与专辑制作工程。 Birdy虽包办所有歌曲创作，却也邀请多位杰出的音乐前辈跨刀来为自己的歌曲灌注新鲜氛围，打造出一首首让挑剔耳朵都能臣服的完美创作，包括：率先拿下英国iTunes单曲榜#22以及台湾KKBOX冠军的首支单曲「Keeping Your Head Up」与「Hear You Calling」可以见到Steve Mac及Wayne Hector (Westlife)赋予磅礡气场与超高流行度；曾为Snow Patrol乐团合音伴奏的Britpop乐团Vega4主唱Johnny McDaid与Birdy合写的「Wild Horse」，到位地诠释即使再悲伤也要忍痛斩断那摇摇欲坠的爱情，温柔与坚强并蓄，给你遭遇困境时勇敢面对的勇气；为Lana Del Rey操刀多首冠军曲的Justin Parker在「Deep End」中细腻描写出对变调爱情的内心纠结；Maroon 5创作班底Ammar Malik携手传递渴望得到解救心灵的「Lifted」；首张专辑翻唱曲－英国独立摇滚团队Cherry Ghost的「People Help The People」作词作曲人Simon Aldred，也与Birdy共谱出纯钢琴伴奏扣人心弦的「Unbroken」；与曾为Disclosure, Clean Bandit等人创作歌曲的歌手制作人RØMANS共同谱写，以清澈动人的琴键与弦乐共构出哀伤唯美、最能代表Birdy的专辑同名歌曲「Beautiful Lies」，以及确实将东方异国情调体现在音乐中的专辑第一首歌曲「Growing Pains」。

　　“Beautiful Lies”对于Birdy来说是一个重要阶段代表作，她不避讳这是一张出道至今最贴近她心灵之作，在这个从小女孩一路蜕变为少女再步入成年的阶段，原本就比别人都早熟的Birdy，有着特别敏锐的感受并深入自我探索，进而完成这张代表她脱茧而出的成年之作。 Birdy擅长以叙事的方式将各种形式的感情层层剖析、抑郁却又坚强的音乐灵魂与穿透力十足的动人嗓音交会，唯美的令人成瘾着迷，并以哀伤频率传递出在脆弱中寻觅重新启程的动力。有时人们当下需要的不是灵魂的救赎，而是渴望能有与自己一同沉溺的陪伴，沉浮在黑暗中寻觅光芒，独自找到继续前进的方向，而这就是Birdy她独一无二的音乐特质。

简介

　　Lonely Drifter Karen是一支三人乐团，主唱、主创和吉它手Tanja Frinta来自于奥地利中部，曾经在维也纳和哥德堡都组建过乐队，直到在巴塞罗纳遇到另两位真命天子——西班牙钢琴手Marc Meliá Sobrevias和意大利鼓手Giorgio Menossi后，有关Lonely Drifter Karen的音乐恋情故事，才终于落地又生根、开花又结果。

　　音乐底蕴究竟是什么样子，听听Lonely Drifter Karen的这张《Glass Is Singing》，你就会明白个大概。整张专辑以音乐剧、中欧民谣、钢琴小品这些传统的古典音乐元素为架构，甚至是同样参与的爵士乐在这里面都已经算是年轻人了。而这样的组合，在如今的Indie其实也并不算太新鲜，但多数的效果，却只能是提供出一种花花草草的梦幻氛围，说的不好听一点，其中的一些音乐，也不过就是拿着这些流行音乐中的非主流元素，来捣捣浆糊、卖个新鲜和混淆视听罢了。但Lonely Drifter Karen的这张处子专辑，给人最大的感觉就是这些古典元素在运用的过程中，依然让人感到它们的古意盎然、底蕴扎实，很有一种文化的厚重感，也正是有了这种基础，Lonely Drifter Karen的音乐也就丝毫没有了同时代一些独立音乐那种比较常见的轻浮和轻飘。而Tanja Frinta游历的经历，加上其它两名乐手的文化背影，更让这种中欧的古朴，又多了一种北欧的清新和西欧的浪漫这样的融合，也让专辑在古朴的基础上，又多了一种纯粹的简洁和轻盈的浪漫。

简介

　　Meg Myers in a singer-songwriter from Nashville, Tennessee who has melted and warmed our hearts since her debut in 2011. Since her first album Daughter in the Choir, which was released in 2012, Myers has slowly built momentum and popularity through incredible vocals, well-thought-out lyrics and sheer hard work. In 2013 and 2014, respectively, Myers released two more studio albums and it seems has continued to write and record with the announcement of another masterpiece.

　　According to her twitter page and online music magazine Pass the Aux, Meg Myers new album will be titled Take Me to the Disco and will be released on July 20th. Her fans, however, will not have to wait long as she has already released her first single “Numb.” The song fades in with a simple drumbeat and what appears to be a synth or strings. Soon her signature vocals come in and it feels as if her voice could produce a sweet molasses. As she has stated, the song is about her experience with the major record label Atlantic Records. However, as she wrote the song she realized that, as she says “I dug deeper, I found that it goes back to some early childhood stuff and how shutting down, or escaping, felt like the safer thing to do.”

　　The album will be released under indie label 300 Entertainment in which they have given her what appears to be gree reign on how this album was created. Due to working with 300 Entertainment, Myers was able to employ full string arrangements for six of her songs. All string parts were performed by The Section Quartet.

　　When talking about the album Meg Myers says that “On the surface, I thought I was writing about love loss but I’ve learned it goes much deeper than that,” Myers said. “It’s going back to the child in me that needed to be healed. I’ve always written from a true place, but in getting to know myself better, I’m now writing from an even deeper level of honesty.”

The image shows a library card labeled Boys Don’t Cry with numerous stamps, implying various due dates. The dates begin with July 2, 2015 and conclude with July 2016 and November 13, 2016. Frank’s brother, Ryan Breaux, further suggested this release with an Instagram caption of the same library card photo reading “BOYS DON’T CRY #JULY2016”.On August 1, 2016, a live video hosted by Apple Music showing an empty hall was launched on the website boysdontcry.co. The website also featured a new “boysdontcry” graphic. The video marked the first update on the website since a “date due” post from July.

　　On August 1, 2016, a video appeared that showed Frank Ocean woodworking and sporadically playing instrumentals on loop. That same day, many news outlets reported that August 5, 2016, could be the release date for Boys Don’t Cry. The video was revealed to be promotion for Endless, a 45-minute long visual album that began streaming on Apple Music on August 19, 2016.[16]

　　The following day, Frank posted a new picture on his website advertising four pop-up stores in Los Angeles, New York, Chicago, and London. Later in the day, the album went up on iTunes.
