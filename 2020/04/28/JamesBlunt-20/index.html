简介

　　2006 CD single taken from the album Back To Bedlam. James Blunt’s voice is delicate, emotional and quite unique. His song writing talent is clear from the start. High, the fourth single from his Back To Bedlam album talks of an all-consuming, passionate love and beautiful dawns, painting beautiful images of both, and delivered in the unmistakable vocal style that we’re sure to hear a lot more of in the future.
