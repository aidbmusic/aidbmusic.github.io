简介

　　不论中外，吉他唱作人路线有如历久不衰依然受尽青睐，近年他方的英国陆续有不少新鲜上阵的唱作型女歌手，今次有被誉为“新Amy Winehouse”的Adele。年仅19岁的Adele生於英国Tottenham，自幼受爵士乐薰陶，如Ella Fitzgerald和Etta Jame都是她陪伴她成长的“音乐老师”。早于14岁已开始作曲，及后毕业于著名表演艺术学府BRIT School，亦即于Kate Nash、Amy Winehouse和Katie Melua等人份属校友。毕业后曾跟随不少音乐人作巡演乐手，如Jamie T、Amos Lee和Keren Ann等。

　　07年底分别推出了两张细碟《Hometown Glory》和《Chasing Pavements》，直至本月底首张专辑《19》才上市。尽管年纪轻轻，但她的唱腔并不幼嫩，混合骚灵的技巧及具爆发力的演译，唱功不容忽视。有人认为她的声线像Dusty Springfield和Jill Scott，那略带低沉而沙哑的嗓子颇适合唱爵士和蓝调。

　　据她所说这张专辑是关于18至19岁年青人的爱情，是一张比较失落的唱片。但从单曲《Chasing Pavements》和《Hometown Glory》的旋律中，渗透出的成熟韵味，并非年轻爱侣的小情小爱。

　　《Daydreamer》中以Acoustic吉他作伴奏，刻意放轻的唱腔结合旋律流露出简朴之美。同类型的《Melt My Heart To Stone》则较具节奏感，那充满感情的演译，更为动听。

　　喜欢写诗的她，在写歌词的时候亦有渗进其中，尽显才情。当Amy Winehouse过著荒唐日子之时，且看她会否藉此机会攀上更高位置。

　　Adele怀着极其复杂的心情公布了她的全新片 “21”，这张新专辑将明年1月24日在英国地区发行，二月底在北美地区发行。随着发行专辑前，新专辑里第一首单曲 “Rolling In the Deep” 会于1月16日公布，这张新专辑大部分录制工作是在美国 Malibu 完成，并与传奇性唱片制作人Rick Rubin（ Johnny Cash, Jay Z, Red Hot Chilli Peppers ）一同合作，后期是她回到伦敦后与制作人Paul Epworth ( Plan B, Bloc Party, Florence )负责完成, 按照Adele自己的方式，这是一张有着忧郁的布鲁斯、福音和Disco的风格作品。 对于新专辑她如此说道，“关于‘21’,我感觉非常兴奋、紧张、热烈渴望甚至也有些焦虑，但很开心在这里向大家公布，我花了很长一段时间。新专辑和两年前的‘19’会有所不同，因为我现在处理的创作方式和心态也不同于以前，我现在变得更有耐心，更诚恳，更宽恕也更能意识到自己的不足，一些个人习性和原则所在。我想一些事情也是伴随着年龄而不断发生的，所以新专辑贴切的命名为‘21’，我的第一专辑以‘19’命名原因也是如此，像是关于我自己的一个目录一样，所有发生在自己身上的一切事物，就像是以照片的方式一样在记录着我个人这些年来的成长发展和改变。” 这两年来她也听取了来自许多不同音乐人的作品，特别是像Wanda Jackson, Allison Krauss, Yvonne Fair, Andrew Bird, Neko Case等。有关于专辑“21”的创作灵感，她还列下了一长串的音乐人名字表示对他们的热烈敬爱，像Mary J Blige, Mos Def, Elbow, Tom Waits, Kanye West, Alanis Morissette和Sinead O’Connor等等

简介

　　21 is the second studio album by English singer-songwriter Adele. Released on 21 January 2011 in the UK and most of Europe, and on 22 February 2011 in North America, the album was named after the age of the singer at the time of its production. While it relies heavily on the folk and Motown influences that framed her debut 19, the album also explores various genres and styles from country and blues to alternative pop, indie-rock, and R&B. 21 is a break-up album, with songs exploring aspects of the singer’s most recently ended relationship.

　　The majority of the album’s production was handled by Grammy Award-winning American producer Rick Rubin, with additional producers including Jim Abbiss, Paul Epworth, Fraser T. Smith, Ryan Tedder and Dan Wilson. Released to positive reviews, critics complimented Adele’s vocal performance and introspective songwriting. Preceded by lead single “Rolling in the Deep”, 21 debuted at number one on the UK Album Chart with first-week sales of 208,000 copies. It also debuted at number one in over ten countries, including Canada, Germany and Ireland. In the U.S., the album debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart, selling 352,000 copies in its first week.

　　Following a well-received performance of the album’s second single “Someone Like You” at the 2011 BRIT Awards, the album maintained its number one position in the UK for eleven consecutive weeks, before being dethroned by the Foo Fighters’ Wasting Light on the week of 17 April 2011. However, 21 returned to the top spot the following week, and as of July 2011, has spent a total of seventeen aggregate weeks at number one. The album’s eleven-week consecutive run at the top broke the record for the longest running consecutive number-one studio album for a solo artist. It also became the biggest-selling digital album of all-time in the UK. 21 has been certified 10-times platinum by the British Phonographic Industry, denoting domestic shipments in excess of three million units. A third single, “Set Fire to the Rain”, attained success across several European territories and has peaked at number eighty-eight on Billboard thus far. In the U.S., the album spent ten non-consecutive weeks at number one, and has sold over 2.6 million copies. Total worldwide sales have surpassed eight million copies, making 21 the best-selling album of the year so far.

Rumour Has It

信息

表演者: Adele

流派: 流行

专辑类型: 单曲

介质: 数字(Digital)

发行时间: 2011

出版者: XL

豆瓣评分: 8.7

简介

　　“Rumour Has It” is a song by the English singer-songwriter Adele from her second studio album, <21>. The song was written by Adele and Ryan Tedder. The song is the fourth single of the album in the Netherlands.

　　“Rumour Has It” is a percussion-driven Jazz number that Adele described as a “bluesy pop stomping song”. She explained that the song was not inspired by the media, but was aimed at her own friends, who frequently spread rumours about her break-up with her boyfriend. “Rumour Has It” contrasts with the second Tedder-collaborated song, and the album’s third track, “Turning Tables”, produced by Jim Abbiss. The song was also promoted on ABC Family’s The Lying Game. American singer Jeremih covered the song on Billboard’s Mashup Mondays.

　　Despite not being released as a single, “Rumour Has It” has charted on various formats, including the Rock Songs chart peaking at #28 and the Triple A chart, where it reached the top position for a week. It has also charted on the Billboard Hot 100 at #96.
