Tonight Not Again Jason Mraz Live at the Eagles Ba

信息

表演者: Jason Mraz

介质: Audio CD

发行时间: 2004-08-24

唱片数: 1

条形码: 0075596296526

豆瓣评分: 9.5

简介

　　Thanks to the platinum success of his debut album Waiting For My Rocket To Come, Jason Mraz became the artist development story of the year in 2003. Released in late 2002, that acclaimed album continued growing throughout 2003 on the strength of two hits - “The Remedy (I Won’t Worry)” & “You & I Both” - & the effusive word-of-mouth surrounding Jason’s brilliant, exhilarating & often hilarious live concert performances. & now that charm & excitement has been captured in its entirety on Tonight, Not Again: Jason Mraz Live At The Eagles Ballroom, a very special (and specially priced) two disc set including an audio CD of his concert from Milwaukee’s Eagles Ballroom, plus a video DVD of the concert plus tons of backstage & behind-the-scenes Mraz goodness. Besides favorites like “The Remedy,” “You & I Both” & a ripping rendition of “Curbside Prophet” featuring John Popper, the album includes live versions of five previously unreleased Mraz tunes.
